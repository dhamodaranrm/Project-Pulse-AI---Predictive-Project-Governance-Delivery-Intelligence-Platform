
# Risk Assessment Documentation

## Overview

This folder contains the risk management and assessment artifacts used for **ProjectPulse AI – Predictive Delivery Intelligence Platform**.

The purpose of these documents is to identify, assess, monitor, and mitigate risks that may impact product delivery, adoption, performance, and business outcomes.

---

## Documents Included

### Risk Assessment Register

Captures identified risks, impact, likelihood, mitigation strategies, and ownership.

### Risk Mitigation Plan

Defines actions to reduce, monitor, or eliminate identified risks throughout the product lifecycle.

### Risk Review Log

Tracks risk status, mitigation progress, and periodic risk assessments.

---

## Key Deliverables

- Risk Identification Framework
- Risk Assessment Register
- Risk Mitigation Strategy
- Risk Monitoring & Review Process

---

## Key Outcomes

- Early identification of delivery and product risks
- Improved decision-making and planning
- Reduced impact of project uncertainties
- Increased delivery confidence
- Better stakeholder visibility into potential risks
