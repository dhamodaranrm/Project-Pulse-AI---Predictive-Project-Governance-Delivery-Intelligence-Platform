
# Agile Workflows Documentation

## Overview

This folder contains the Agile delivery framework, workflows, and planning artifacts used for the development and continuous improvement of **ProjectPulse AI – Predictive Delivery Intelligence Platform**.

The purpose of these documents is to establish a structured approach for managing requirements, planning releases, executing sprints, tracking progress, and ensuring successful product delivery.

---

## Documents Included

### Sprint Workflow Documentation

Defines the end-to-end sprint lifecycle, including backlog refinement, sprint planning, daily stand-ups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives.

### Sprint Planning Templates

Provides a standardized structure for sprint planning, estimation, prioritization, and delivery tracking.

### Project Status Reporting

Captures delivery progress, risks, dependencies, blockers, and key milestones to ensure stakeholder visibility and governance.
