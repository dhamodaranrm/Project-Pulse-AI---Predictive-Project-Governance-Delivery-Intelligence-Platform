
# BRD & PRD Documentation

## Overview

This folder contains the Business Requirements Document (BRD) and Product Requirements Document (PRD) for **ProjectPulse AI – Predictive Delivery Intelligence Platform**.

These documents define the business objectives, product vision, user needs, functional capabilities, AI requirements, and success criteria that guide the design and delivery of the product.

---

## Documents Included

### Business Requirements Document (BRD)

The BRD defines:

- Business Problem
- Business Goals & Objectives
- Stakeholders
- Business Scope
- Expected Outcomes
- Business Benefits
- Risks & Constraints
- Success Metrics

**Purpose:** Establish the business justification for the product and define the value it delivers to the organization.

---

### Product Requirements Document (PRD)

The PRD defines:

- Product Vision
- Product Goals
- User Personas
- Functional Requirements
- AI Capabilities
- Non-Functional Requirements
- Product Success Criteria

**Purpose:** Translate business needs into product capabilities and detailed requirements.
